Privacy Policy – KIITO

KIITO is an unofficial student utility application designed to help users access attendance and timetable information in a convenient manner.

Information We Access

The app requires users to enter institutional login credentials (such as roll number and password) solely to authenticate with the official institutional portal.

  • No credentials are stored permanently on external servers controlled by the developer
  • No personal data is sold or shared with third parties
  • No advertising SDKs are used
  • No tracking technologies are used

Data Usage

All data retrieved from institutional systems is used only to display information requested by the user inside the app.

Third-Party Services

The app communicates directly with the official institutional website to fetch attendance and timetable data. The developer does not control those systems.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect transmitted information. However, users should protect their credentials and avoid sharing them.

Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for enrolled students of the institution and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

This policy may be updated when necessary. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance.
